    Web games / Dodgeball

    Throw. Dodge. Survive.

    A simplified browser version of the first game I ever built — originally written in Lua with drawn characters. Throw balls at your opponent and dodge theirs. First player to lose all three health points loses.

    Back to Web Games
    Controls

    How to play

    Both players share the keyboard. Throw to attack, dash to dodge quickly in your movement direction.

    Player 1 — Blue (left)

    • Move W / S
    • Throw D
    • Dash A

    Player 2 — Red (right)

    • Move ↑ / ↓
    • Throw ←
    • Dash →

    Rules

    Each player starts with 3 health points. A ball that hits the opponent removes one. Balls bounce off the top and bottom walls, but disappear if they reach either side edge. Throw and dash both have a short cooldown shown in the HUD. Press Space or click the canvas to restart after a round ends.

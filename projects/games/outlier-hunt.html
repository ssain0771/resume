    How scoring works

    Each outlier found scores 100 points plus a time bonus. Finding them quickly earns more. Wrong clicks drain the clock by three seconds each.

    How difficulty scales

    Clusters get tighter and the timer shorter each round. From round four onward, two outliers appear per round instead of one.

    What is an outlier?

    In data analysis, an outlier is an observation that sits far from the bulk of the data. Identifying them quickly is a core skill in any clustering or anomaly detection workflow.
